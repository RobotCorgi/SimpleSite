Library of Congress

Located in Washington D.C.. The national library of the United States of America, the Library of Congress was founded in 1800. It is officially the largest library in the world. It has over 170 million items. Items available at the Library of Congress are extremely varied. Items ranging from daguerreotypes, music, maps, films, and of course, books.
