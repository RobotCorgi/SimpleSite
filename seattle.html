Seattle Central Library

This branch of the Seattle Public Library is located in Seattle, Washington. The first official Seattle Public Library opened in 1890, but it sadly burnt down in 1901. This location, the Seattle Central Library, opened on May 23, 2004. Designed by architects Rem Koolhaas and Joshua Prince-Ramus. It features a modern design with 11 stories of steel and glass.
